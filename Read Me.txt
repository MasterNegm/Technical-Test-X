Technical Design Document
*Game Idea
Call to arms is a snake game with different theme as you play by warriors trying to recruit more warriors to win the battle 
againist golems and other creatures(in future version we can put different kinds of monsters(Obstacles) with different mechanics like moving Obstacle and chaser Obstacle....)

*Game Features
2 levels
Pc & Mobile Controls
